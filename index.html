Beano - Fun Force

The professional project module consisted of different disciplinaries to form a team and create a minimum viable product for a client. We got given a brief set by Beano studios. They wanted us to makre a game that is inspired from marioware and the mario party games. We chose to follow the same path of mario party and create and implement a game with an overworld, a series of minigames and a score system. We developed our game in Unity and we took on a minigame each and some of the menu/overworld mechanics. My minigame was a concept inspired by angry birds where the player would throw pies at the enemies going past the screen. This project gave us a real understanding of how a development company should operate and how to set realistic deadlines. Our client worked very closely with us which was a create help in driving the game in the direction they wanted.

Grade: B+
